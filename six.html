Dil

tere samne ane se mera ye Dil kamzor ho jata hai,
Tujhe awaaj dena chahta hai fir bhi khamosh ho jata hai

Mujhse baate ho ya na ho teri iss baat ka malal nhi,
Phir bhi na janee dusro se jara hasle tu
To ye udaas ho jata hai

Mujse baate ho teri ya na ho
Tera sath hona hi mujhe sukoon de jata hai
Kambhakt hai ye dil hamara mohabbat krta hai,
Phir bhi jatane se darta hai

Apko lgta hum darte hai apse,
Galat hai ap mera ye dil tujhe khone se darta hai
