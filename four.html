Shauk

Shauk nhi to shauk paida krke to dekh,
Tere wafa ke liye rone wale ko dil mai basakar to dekh

Mana hai aib mujhme hajaro,
Tujhse kitni wafa krta hu uski gehrayi to dekh

Mile honge tujhe rasto mai bichadne wale log,
Tu muqaddar ke dar se mere mohabbat se mehroom na rehna,

Maut to likhi hai o to ani hai
Maut ke dar se,
Tere sath jannat mai rhne ki khawaish krne wale ki mohabbat se tu mehroom na rehna..
