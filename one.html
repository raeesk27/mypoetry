Puch ke to dekh le....

Tujhe kyu , kitna, kis qadar chahata hu ekbaar puchke to dekhle
Kbhi tere dil ke diwaane ko dil mai basakar to dekhle

Ha mana pasand nhi hai tujhe mera tujhko chahna
ek Baar chahne ki wajah puchke to dekhle

Ye jo dil ke qabar maine dafan kiyi hai baate,
Kbhi waqt mile to is qabar par salam krke to dekhle
Kbhi tere chahne Wale se ek baar tu chahne ki wajah puchkar to dekhle

Ha mana tu ana nhi chahti mere dil mai
Tere dil mai na akar koi khuda se teri Milne ki dua mangta ho to bata Dena
Namajo mai uthkar tere liye koi rota ho to bata dena

Sone se pehle uthne ke baad koi tere khayalo mai khota ho to bata Dena
Teri udaasi, ghusssa, taklif, Tere ankhon mai dekhkar koi pehchanta ho to bata dena

Tujhe samne dekhkar kisike dil, dimaag
Khamosh ho jate ho to bata Dena
Tujhse baat krte waqt kisika dil teji se dhadakta ho to bata dena
Tera likha khat dekh koi bechain hota ho to bata dena

Daulat, shaurat bohot se denge tujhe
Meri jaisi deewangi koi de to bata dena

Tere Surat ke chahne Wale bohot se milenge tujhe,
Mere jaisa koi tere rooh ka diwana ho to bata dena

Mohabaat ko meri jata nahi pata magar
Aisi deewangi lekar koi tujhse mohabbat kre to bata dena

Lagti hongi meri ye deewangi logo ko pagalpan,
Maine is pagalpan ko Teri mohabbat ka naam Diya
Chanlena pura jahaan Mere jaisa koi chahne wala mil jaye to bata dena...
