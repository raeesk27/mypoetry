Ankhein

Is khudagrzi ke jamane mai , raatke andhero mai jugnu sa koi mila hai
Mere gam k bagiche me muskurahat sa jo khila h

Kafi surate dekhi is jindagi ke baajar mai lekin jo de na saka koi o sukoon use dekhke mila hai

Hum to tabaah hone ki raah par hi nikle the,
Firse jine ki wajah dene wala koi mila hai

Shayad koi layak nhi mere irdgird jo mujhe apna sake,

Isliye khuda ne tujhe muhjhse kuch khas maqsad se milaya hai

Sabse alag, Kohinoor sa
Dekh ke use chand Sharma jaye aisa humsafar mila hai...
